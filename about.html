About Me

I’m a Data Engineer focused on building reliable, traceable data pipelines and analytics-ready datasets that teams can trust. I enjoy turning messy, semi-structured data into clean relational models, and designing warehouse structures that make reporting faster and decisions clearer.

My strengths sit at the intersection of ETL engineering, T-SQL performance, and cloud-aligned data modelling. I’ve worked with API-driven ingestion, JSON/XML normalization, and building curated datasets for BI tools—while keeping quality and governance in mind.

I also bring strong delivery discipline from project environments: stakeholder communication, documentation, and structured execution— the things that help data work move from “it runs” to “it delivers value.”
